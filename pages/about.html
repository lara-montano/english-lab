Mission

What is English Lab?

English Lab is a free, self-paced digital learning platform created for Engineering students at UAQ who need to develop their English proficiency — whether for everyday communication, academic coursework, or TOEFL certification.

All materials are curated and designed to be practical, challenging, and directly relevant to the academic and professional contexts that Engineering students encounter.

  • No registration or downloads required
  • Works on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop
  • Covers General, Academic, and TOEFL English
  • Interactive exercises with immediate feedback
  • Regularly updated with new content
