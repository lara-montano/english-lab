About

Designed for UAQ Engineering students

English Lab was created to give every student at the Facultad de Ingeniería access to high-quality, structured English materials — regardless of their starting level.

  • Free access — no registration required
  • Works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer
  • Organized from beginner to advanced level
  • Aligned with TOEFL iBT requirements for UAQ
  • Updated regularly with new exercises and guides
