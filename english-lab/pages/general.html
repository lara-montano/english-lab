Travel Vocabulary

  • •departure — the act of leaving; "Our departure is at 6 AM."
  • •itinerary — a planned route or journey; "Check the itinerary for stops."
  • •accommodation — a place to stay; "We booked accommodation near the airport."
  • •currency — the money system of a country; "Exchange your currency before arriving."
  • •customs — border control; "Declare items at customs."
  • •layover — a stop between flights; "We have a 3-hour layover in Houston."
💼

Workplace Vocabulary

  • •deadline — final date for completion; "The deadline is Friday."
  • •colleague — a person you work with; "Ask a colleague for help."
  • •agenda — list of meeting topics; "Check the agenda before the meeting."
  • •remote work — working from home; "Remote work is now common in tech."
  • •feedback — evaluation of work; "Ask for feedback on your report."
  • •negotiate — to discuss terms; "We negotiated the contract terms."
